Frequently Asked Questions

What technologies do you work with?
I primarily use C++ and Java for backend as well as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for front end.
Describe an instance when you disagreed with a teammate.
We were working on a project for a data structures class but we were having scheduling issues because not everyone was in town at the time so we had to work towards a solution. One of my teammates suggested that we wouldn't need to meet up at all and everything could be completed through texts but I disagreed. I communicated with my teammate about the problem so that we both understand what the problem that needs to be addressed. I suggested that a meeting can happen as long as 2 or more members are available so that we could communicate better and others can join later. We eventually reached a mutual agreement and completed the project on time.
Share an example of a failure or setback.
When I took my first college calculus class, I was overwhelmed by the workload and the amount of materials so I did not do well on my first midterm and I needed a decent GPA to transfer later. So I decided to fix my study habits, attend office hours, and seek help from tutoring services. In the end, I got an A in the class, which proved that thinking of solutions to adapt to a new environment is extremely important.
